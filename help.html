You don't have to sit with the noise alone.

If things feel overwhelming right now, you don't need to wait for a therapy session to reach out. These services are free, confidential, and available 24/7 in the UK.

Samaritans

Whatever you're going through, a Samaritan will face it with you.

Call Free 24/7 116 123

Shout

Confidential text support for anyone struggling to cope.

Text Free 24/7 SHOUT to 85258

Childline

Support for anyone under 19 with any issue they're going through.

Call Free 24/7 0800 1111
