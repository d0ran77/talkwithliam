The
Library.

A curated collection of essential reading on Transactional Analysis, script formation, and the human condition.

Games
People
Play

Eric Berne

Games People Play

Eric Berne (1964)

The foundational text of Transactional Analysis. It exposes the unconscious social 'games' we play to avoid genuine intimacy, offering a stark but necessary look at how we interact to reinforce our early scripts.

I'm OK
-
You're OK

Thomas A. Harris

I'm OK - You're OK

Thomas A. Harris (1967)

A highly accessible entry point into understanding your ego states. It breaks down the four core life positions we hold and how to consciously shift towards a position of mutual respect and Adult autonomy.

Find a Copy →

The Body
Keeps
The Score

Bessel van der Kolk

The Body Keeps The Score

Bessel van der Kolk (2014)

While not strictly TA, this is essential reading for understanding how early trauma lives in our physiology. It validates the physical sensations of anxiety and anger we often carry from childhood survival adaptations.

TA
Today

I. Stewart & V. Joines

TA Today

Ian Stewart & Vann Joines (1987)

The gold-standard modern textbook for Transactional Analysis. It breaks down the entire theoretical framework into clear, accessible language, making it brilliant for both students and clients wanting to deepen their understanding.
